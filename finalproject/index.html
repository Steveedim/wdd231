President of Nigeria

Name: Bola Ahmed Tinubu

Bola Ahmed Tinubu, born in 1952, is the current President of Nigeria, having assumed office on May 29, 2023. He previously served as the Governor of Lagos State from 1999 to 2007. A prominent figure in Nigerian politics, he co-founded the Action Congress party and played a key role in the formation of the All Progressives Congress (APC), the ruling party.

About Nigeria

Nigeria is a diverse country in West Africa with a federal system made up of 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory (Abuja). It is home to hundreds of ethnic groups and hundreds of native languages.
